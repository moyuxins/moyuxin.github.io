Online telemedicine service and Delivery service

Although the self-isolation means are honorable, we must voice our concerns to patients who have chronic diseases. Patients with such diseases must receive their medication periodically, otherwise risks furthering their ailments and thus crowding the hospitals in the long run.Online telemedicine service is here to help patients looking to avoid both infection exposure risk and prevent furthering chronic diseases.And we will provide medicine delivery service.

The role of 24/7 online medical

Whether it is the problem of how to reduce the risk of new coronavirus infection, or the problem of exacerbation of chronic diseases caused by discontinuation of medication, online medical care can be easily solved. For patients who are unable to visit medical institutions due to busyness or other reasons, this website provides online medical services from 9:00 to 20:00 every day, all year round.
